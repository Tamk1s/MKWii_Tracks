This folder handles BRSAR sfx/jingles, and BRSTM music.

Folder breakdown:

"Source"	- Original source audio files, untouched
"Input"		- Formatted files, ready to use for converting into BRSTM or into a SFX slot in the BRSAR
"Output"	- Output BRSAR (new sfx, BRSTM lengths patched), new BRSTM files

Tools needed for this folder:
BrawlBox (WAV->BRSTM conversion, importing WAV into GRP SFX slots)
Audacity (Project files)
Patch that BRSAR! (http://wiki.tockdom.com/wiki/Patch_that_BRSAR!)
